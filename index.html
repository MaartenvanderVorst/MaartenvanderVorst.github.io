Antenna Workshop

The Antenna & Sub-Millimetre Waves Section of the Radio Frequency Payloads & Technology Division organises the 40th ESA Antenna Workshop from 8-10 October 2019. This workshop will provide an overview of recent and on-going antenna and microwave instrument developments with particular focus on items installed on terrestrial and small-space platforms, including CubeSats/PocketQube, High-Altitude Platforms and Unmanned Aerial Vehicles.
