Antenna Workshop

The Antenna & Sub-Millimetre Waves Section of the Radio Frequency Payloads & Technology Division organises the 40th ESA Antenna Workshop from 8-10 October 2019. This workshop will provide an overview of recent and on-going antenna and microwave instrument developments with particular focus on items installed on terrestrial and small-space platforms, including CubeSats/PocketQube, High-Altitude Platforms and Unmanned Aerial Vehicles. INSTALL APP (iOS/ANDROID)

To install the app just go to the App Store or the Google Play Store and search for Antenna Workshop.

CREATE ACCOUNT (with PIN)

When the app is installed you get to the login window. For the first time you have to select “Create an Account”. Fill in a valid email address because this will be used to reset the password when needed. Then put your password in but you have to start with the 4-digit PIN that was send to you by mail. This PIN is only needed to create the account and later for the login only the real password is needed.
 

WORKSHOP INFO

Once logged in the app the info screen is shown. Here are tabs for exhibitors, social diner and messages. The live messages are used to send updates and other important information like programme updates or changes to schedule. To reload the messages just pull down to refresh.

WORKSHOP PROGRAMME

To see the workshop programme you have to click the speaker icon in the top. This opens a new window with the sessions for Tue/Wed and Thu. Colours (Green, Orange, Grey) are used to indicate the status. Green is finished, Orange is ongoing and Grey is still to come. To refresh the status, please pull down to refresh. In the tab “Agenda” you can find the papers that you have added to your own calendar. To add a paper to your calendar just click on a paper and click the calendar icon. You can also remove the paper from the calendar is a similar way.

WORKSHOP VOTING

For each paper you have the option to give your anonymous vote. The votes are between 1 and 5 are saved to the cloud without sharing user information. All votes will be collected and then used to support the Organising Committee in selecting the Best Paper Award and Best Innovation Award. Of course, your vote can be changed until the end.  

DOWNLOAD PROGRAMME

To download the latest programme (also needed after creating the account) click the button on the top left next to “Workshop Sessions”. To make sure the screens are refreshed pull down for each day separately.
